Who We Are

A Different Kind
of Financial Partner

NovaPlusBank started with a simple idea: banking should work for the people who use it, not the other way around. What began as a small group of employees pooling their savings has grown into one of the region's most trusted credit unions.

As a member-owned cooperative, every decision we make is guided by what's best for you — not outside shareholders. That means lower fees, better rates, and a team that actually knows your name.

  • Member-owned, not shareholder-driven
  • FDIC insured deposits up to $250,000
  • Serving communities across the Northeast

Our Journey

Milestones That Matter

Key moments in our growth from a small credit union to a regional leader.

1987

Founded as a Employee Credit Union

A group of 200 local employees pooled $50,000 to create a better banking alternative.

1998

Community Charter Expansion

Opened membership to all residents in the tri-state area, reaching 10,000 members.

2010

Digital Banking Launch

Introduced online and mobile banking, making member accounts accessible 24/7.

2020

Surpassed 50,000 Members

Crossed $2.5 billion in assets under management with 12 full-service branches.

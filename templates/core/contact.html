Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How quickly will I hear back?

We aim to respond to all form submissions and emails within one business day. Urgent matters are handled immediately via our 24/7 phone line.

Can I schedule a branch appointment?

Walk-ins are always welcome. For mortgage consultations or business banking meetings, mention your preferred time in the message field and we'll confirm by email.

I'm not a member yet — can I still contact you?

Absolutely. Our advisors are happy to answer questions about membership, rates, and products before you open an account.

How do I report a lost or stolen card?

Call 1-800-BANKING immediately — available 24/7. Do not use the contact form for time-sensitive security issues.
